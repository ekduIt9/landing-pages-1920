ABOUT US

Founded in 2014, Illuminati Productions quickly became a leading team acquired many achievements in Film Production industry in vietnam, especially in the northern market.

All team members, who have youth and passion, always believe that working diligently and confidentially is vital to the journey of creating ART.

After long periods of working, experiencing and developing, FMN from Illuminati proudly enters the
market with professional knowledge, perfect workflows, enthusiastic working attitudes, etc. We are eagle to create ART as well as to meet our customers expectations.

MISSION & VISION
OUR TEAM
MISSION
FMN from Illuminati is comprised of a committed group of young professionals who have an intense enthusiasm and devotion to film and other visual media, as well as a desire to work together to tell stories of significance. We aspire to produce films that are a form of art - not merely to entertain, but to provoke emotion and reflection, incite action, and have long-lasting effects on those who experience them.
VISION
The Vietnamese film industry represents a new phase in its development by making a step towards productions that meet the demands of the international market. A precondition for the success of this strategic step is the development of production stages with an production management. FM N from Illuminati longs to companion with brands, artists, and pictures... to together affirm our position in the market and becomes a ClassA/top Production house within the country and even worldwide.
